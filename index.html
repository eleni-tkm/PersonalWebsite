Hi, Welcome!

Hey visitor! Welcome to my personal site. This is Eleni, a geology enthusiast with a twist of tech-savviness. I started off by studying Geology at the University of Patras, then went on to get a Master's in Applied Geophysics. Along the way, I discovered my love for tools like MATLAB and Python, which I use to solve all sorts of cool problems during my studies. After a stint as a junior geophysicist, I switched gears and became an IT manager, focusing mainly on geoinformatics. Lately, I've been diving into web programming, because I just can't get enough of learning new things.
